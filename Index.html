Dr. Srinivas Kota, MD, FMNM

Dr. Srinivas Kota is a board-certified Internal Medicine physician and Diplomate of the American Board of Obesity Medicine, Metabolic and Nutritional Medicine. With over 15 years of clinical experience, he specializes in women's hormonal health, menopause management, and metabolic medicine, helping women across the United States achieve optimal wellness.

Dr. Kota's approach to menopause treatment combines the latest medical research with personalized patient care. He understands that every woman's menopause experience is unique and requires individualized treatment strategies that address not just symptoms, but overall quality of life.

Through his telehealth practice, Dr. Kota has made expert menopause care accessible to women nationwide, offering convenient consultations and ongoing support throughout their treatment journey.
